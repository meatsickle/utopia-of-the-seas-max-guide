The four-step cruise algorithm

1
Before sailingReserve your headliner shows, lock one premium meal if wanted, and decide whether Nassau is beach club or walkable DIY.
2
Embarkation dayUse the quiet early window for waterslides, FlowRider, ship photos, and any app cleanup while most guests are still boarding.

Port daysGo all-in on the plan you picked. Half-committing wastes both time and energy on a short itinerary.

Sea day closeoutCash in remaining backlog: one big show, one big dinner, one calm recovery zone, and any missed attractions.

Do this on day 1

Ask early, not after a problem

Free if you ask beats paid if you panic

Service can vary by sailing

These are recurring cruiser-reported wins, not guaranteed entitlements. Ask nicely, ask early, and adapt to the crew’s actual capacity.

2026 Dining Highlights

Complimentary Standouts

  • Solarium Bistro (Deck 14) — Breakfast/lunch/dinner, Mediterranean vibe, often empty while Windjammer is slammed. All-ages, natural light, plated options.
  • Park Café (Deck 8 Central Park) — Quick paninis, salads, Royal Kummelweck. Great for fast grab-and-go.
  • El Loco Fresh (Deck 16) — Tacos, burritos, nachos with salsa bar. Fast and free.
  • The Spare Tire (pool deck food truck) — Tornados, chicken sandwiches, mini cheesecake churros.
  • Room service continental breakfast = FREE (hot items have fee).
    • Specialty Splurges (Updated Pricing)

    Worth It or Skip?

  • Royal Railway – Utopia Station (Deck 4) — Immersive Wild West train dinner/show. ~$75–$120. Multi-sensory, short rib, poussin, “Fart & Dart” beans. Book early — unique to this ship.
  • Chops Grille / 150 Central Park — ~$55–70 dinner. Steakhouse or elevated tasting. Lunch versions cheaper if available.
  • Izumi Hibachi or Omakase — Hibachi ~$45–65, Omakase 8-course ~$130. Good for groups.
  • Mason Jar — Southern comfort, fried chicken, shrimp & grits. Brunch ~$35 on sea days. Live music vibe.
  • Unlimited Dining Package — Often discounted pre-cruise; covers most specialty for the short sailing.
    • Hack: Specialty lunch is frequently half the dinner price with same quality. Check app day-of for openings.
