Tips + Secrets

Pre-cruise setup, money-saving moves, crowd-avoidance patterns, and hidden-activity notes from the original tips pages.

Before You Board

  • Download the Royal Caribbean app and check in early.
  • Book Aqua80Too, All In!, and Youtopia as soon as the window opens.
  • Pre-purchase Wi-Fi, drinks, and shore items when possible.
  • Bring a lanyard for SeaPass use.
  • Pack sneakers for zip line, rock wall, and select excursions.

Save Money

  • Free motion-sickness meds are available at Guest Services.
  • Embarkation-day spa pricing can be the better play.
  • Free basic coffee beats Starbucks if all you need is caffeine.
  • Specify liquor brands with package drinks if it matters to you.
  • Swim diapers are available onboard if needed.

Beat Crowds

  • Slides and surf during dinner hours can be much better.
  • Windjammer is easiest before 7:30 AM and after 1:30 PM.
  • Pool deck clears some during headline show windows.
  • Staying onboard for one port block can make the ship feel empty.
  • Destination elevators help if you learn the flow early.

Hidden Gem Activities

  • Sea Ducks app for the unofficial duck hunt.
  • Card room for quiet downtime.
  • Art auction if you just want the vibe and free bubbly.
  • Ask about behind-the-scenes or galley/bridge tour options.
  • Silent disco and stargazing both punch above their effort.
Most important pattern This ship is built for sensory overload on a short itinerary. The best outcome comes from early reservations, early island starts, and using the calmer hidden zones as recovery points instead of wasting time deciding where to go next.

Contests + Games

Not official answer sheets, but the best useful prep from the contests page plus the trivia-fact angles that tend to matter.

Sea Ducks

Passenger-led duck hiding game. Check railings, tucked corners, decor edges, and hidden public-space spots. Use the Sea Ducks app for hints and logging.

🏀

Sports Competitions

Basketball, ping pong, mini golf, and poolside games. Check Cruise Compass daily and sign up early.

🧩

Escape Rooms

Reserve them on day one. Communicate loudly, split tasks, and treat them as a premium indoor sea-day anchor.

🎰

Casino Tournaments

Slots, blackjack, poker, and promo events can appear. Check with the desk on day one if that matters to your group.

🎤

Karaoke + Love & Marriage

Usually more about fun and energy than prizes. Great if your group wants audience-heavy chaos.

Common trivia-ready facts

  • Utopia is about 1,188 feet long and roughly 236,860 gross tons.
  • Maximum guest count is around 5,668.
  • Daredevil's Peak at CocoCay is the tallest waterslide in the Caribbean.
  • The Bahamas capital is Nassau and USD is widely accepted there.
  • Aqua80Too means 80s music knowledge never hurts.

Duck-hunt etiquette

  • Do not hide ducks in pools or hot tubs.
  • Do not stash them in shops or in Central Park plants.
  • Photos and hints boost engagement.
  • Tag the duck with ship and sailing date.
