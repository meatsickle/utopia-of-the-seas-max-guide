Smart Moves

Three quick matrices for planning decisions before and during the trip.

Book Before Sailing

  • Drink package and Wi-Fi
  • Specialty dining if doing two or more
  • Atlantis, Coco Beach Club, or paid thrill items
  • Early arrival/check-in window
  • Any premium add-on you would be annoyed to miss

Hit Early

  • CocoCay chairs before 8:30 AM
  • Slides and surf right after embarkation
  • Zip line and rock wall on sea morning
  • Escape room signups on day one
  • Aqua80Too seating well before showtime

Save Money

  • Specialty lunch over dinner
  • Free motion sickness meds at Guest Services
  • Continental room service breakfast
  • Skip Starbucks if basic coffee is enough
  • Embarkation-day spa deals can be lower

Premium Targets

  • 150 Central Park: Best upscale finale dinner.
  • Chops Grille: Safe premium choice if your group wants steak.
  • Giovanni's: Good if you want a more scenic, relaxed dinner flow.
  • Izumi: Strong for hibachi energy or quick sushi via the park window.
  • Royal Railway: Built for spectacle first, food plus experience.

Best tactic: lunch pricing is often the cleanest upgrade path if you want the experience without a full dinner spend.

Port Compare

One side is easy urban wandering, the other is engineered island fun. Plan accordingly.

Nassau

Tuesday, June 30 / Docked 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM

Easy Walkables

  • Queen's Staircase
  • Fort Fincastle
  • Straw Market
  • Parliament Square
  • Junkanoo Beach
  • Arawak Cay Fish Fry

Booked Upgrades

  • Atlantis Aquaventure
  • Blue Lagoon Island
  • Paradise Island clubs
  • Snorkeling and ATV tours
  • Animal encounters

Best move: either keep Nassau simple and low-friction, or commit to one strong prebooked excursion. Don't half-do both.
